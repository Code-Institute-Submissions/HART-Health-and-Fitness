HART Health and Fitness is the brain child of Jospeh Morgan. Joe established HART Health and Fitness 8 years ago in Manchester, England. He has now been based in Copenhagan, Denmark for the last 4 years, offering a range of Group Classes and One on One training programmes.

"I want to help you, to get your own determination, motivation and passion for your health and fitness. Using my knowledge and experience, I can help guide you to achieve your goals. We will work together to create a clear goal and a flexible training plan to fit any lifestyle!"

- Joseph Morgan P.T
